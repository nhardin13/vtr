## What's Covered

<div class="page-content">

Your warranty covers:

- **Factory defects** in parts we install
- **Workmanship issues** from Valley Top Repair repairs
- **Equipment damage** caused directly by our repair work
- **Parts failures** within 30 days of installation
- **Professional repairs** by certified technicians

</div>

---

## What's Not Covered

<div class="page-content">

The following are not covered under warranty:

- Normal wear and tear on equipment
- Damage from misuse or lack of maintenance
- Pre-existing conditions not related to our repairs
- Damage from third-party repairs or modifications
- Environmental damage (rust, weather, storage conditions)
- Issues resulting from operator error

</div>

---

## Warranty Process

<div class="page-content">

**Step 1 - Contact Us**: Reach out within 30 days of repair if you experience issues

**Step 2 - Describe the Problem**: Reference your repair ticket and explain what's happening

**Step 3 - Schedule Inspection**: We'll schedule a warranty inspection at no charge

**Step 4 - We Handle It**: We'll repair or replace parts covered under warranty

**Step 5 - Equipment Ready**: Your equipment is returned ready for use

</div>

## Getting Warranty Service

<div class="page-content">

To get warranty service on your equipment:

1. **Keep Your Documents** - Retain your repair receipt or ticket number
2. **Contact Us** - Reach out with your ticket number and issue description
3. **Bring Equipment** - Bring the equipment to our shop for warranty inspection
4. **We'll Take Care of It** - We handle all warranty repairs and replacements
