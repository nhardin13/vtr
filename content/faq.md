## Pricing and Rates

<div class="accordion-item">
<button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq3">
What is your standard service rate?
</button>
<div id="faq3" class="accordion-collapse collapse" data-bs-parent="#faqAccordion">
<div class="accordion-body">
Most of our services are quoted as all-inclusive prices based on equipment type and size. For example, residential push mower tune-ups start at $80, and riding mower tune-ups start at $140. 

For custom or repair work not on our standard pricing list, the rate is $50 per hour plus parts. See our [Pricing page](/pricing) for complete details on all service types.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq4">
Is there a diagnostic or estimate fee?
</button>
<div id="faq4" class="accordion-collapse collapse" data-bs-parent="#faqAccordion">
<div class="accordion-body">
We offer a $35 diagnostic fee to inspect and diagnose your equipment. This fee is **waived if you choose to proceed with the repair**. The diagnostic includes:
- Complete equipment inspection
- Parts lookup and sourcing
- Detailed cost estimate
- Professional recommendation

This ensures you know exactly what you're paying for before any work begins.
</div>
</div>
</div>
